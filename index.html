About Us

We are a group of six hardworking students from UiTM's MC111 programme that are dedicated to reviewing food at our local restaurants. We spend our free time trying new dishes and writing honest assessments of our experiences. From the presentation of the dish to the flavor, we leave no detail unturned in their reviews. We are passionate about finding the best food in our area and sharing our thoughts with you.
