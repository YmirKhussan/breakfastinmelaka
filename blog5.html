MakCik Cafe

Background

MakCik Cafe is a stall in a medan selara. It is located right infront of UiTM Lendu campus. Being UiTM Lendu students, this medan selera is definitely in one of our top 5 list because as with Blackberry Corner's case, it is close to home. And UiTM is like our second home :>

Their Menu

MakCik Cafe's menu offers a wide variety of breakfast dishes that includes goreng2an, nasi lemak, and bakso.

Review

Nasi Lemak
Price: RM6


Nasi Lemak has been a phenomenal main dish in Malaysia since forever. When we use the word phenomenal, we are referring to Nasi Lemak Kerang here at Dapur Arang, an exquisite everyday dish, Nasi Lemak Kerang. Fused with exceptionally balanced sambal with anchovies on the side, making the dish a complete mouth watering presentation to customers. Using banana leaf as a foundation of it's presentation, making it more aromatic paired with the smell of the milky rice, and when it enters the taste buds, it exploded in a harmonious way that we would like to order another plate to experience it for second and third round.

Nasi Goreng Ayam
Price: RM7.50


A mix of breakfast and lunch that you can combine with variety of lauk?. Today, we are reviewing called Makcik Cafe. This place serves breakfast that you can mix with lauk to your liking with a reasonable price. Directly place infront of UiTM Lendu this place is a spot for students and outsider to come eat and chill after class and work. Overall this place we would give it a 7/10

MakCik Cafe has been with us since day 1 - through ups and downs. To rate this stall without being too biased since we have a special connection with this stall, we are giving MakCik Cafe a 4/5 star rating since everything from A to Z is great but the only thing that can be improved by them is their Sirap Beng. Sirap Beng is MY (Amir, the author) favourite drink of all time and it pains me when one of my favourite lepak place could not deliver the most basic drink ever.
