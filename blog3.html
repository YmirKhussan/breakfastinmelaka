Asam Pedas Ridhwan

Background

This one's another of our regular visits since it is relatively close to our campus, UiTM. Asam Pedas Rezqi Ridhwan is a modest stall located near a fire station so we call the place "Kedai Sebelah Bomba". We were first introduced to this stall by our lecturer, Madam Raihan, when she bought nasi lemak for the whole class! Who doesn't enjoy free food?

Their Menu

Kedai Sebelah Bomba's menu consists of comfort Malaysian breakfast dishes. They offer a wide variety of roti choices at an incredibly affordable prices. For our recent visit, we decided to try their nasi lemak with different lauks.

Review

Nasi Lemak Ayam Goreng
Price: RM6


Nasi lemak ayam goreng is like my favourite food ever and I have tasted countless of different variations of it so it is very hard to impress me. In Asam Pedas Rezqi Ridhwan's case, their Nasi lemak ayam goreng is literal perfection! The rice is great, sambal is not too spicy and have that little sweetness which is CRUCIAL. Lastly, the ayam goreng hit the mark as its texture is crunchy on the outside and empuk on the inside. 9/10

Nasi Lemak Rendang
Price: RM7


One of the primary dishes that both Malaysians and foreign visitors frequently choose is Nasi Lemak. The Nasi Lemak menu offers a variety of side dishes, including spicy chicken, sambal sotong, and clam rendang. Don't pass up the chance to experience the distinctiveness of Nasi Lemak at the Ucop stand close to the fire station.

Nasi Lemak Sambal Paru
Price: RM5


Who doesn't love nasi lemak right? Personally, this nasi lemak is one of the best nasi lemak we've ever tasted! The nasi lemak was grainy, perfectly creamy and aromatic. The anchovies and peanuts were crunchy. The sambal was not too spicy and sweet. It was a perfect combination of flavours and textures! Sambal paru was also perfectly cooked and spicy. We would rate this this 10/10.

The name "Asam Pedas Rezqi Ridhwan" is pretty misleading since their signature dishes are Nasi Lemak and Roti Canais so thats minus 1 point. Other than that, the food was delicious and we had a pretty good time enjoying our food next to a calm road on a moody Saturday morning. We are giving Kedai Sebelah Bomba a 4-star rating!
