Blackberry Corner

Background

What better way to start our food hunting journey than to eat somewhere close to home? We mean that literally, close to home. Blackberry Corner is a breakfast restaurant located at the corner of the road we live in, hence the name "Blackberry Corner". Three words that best describe our experience at Blackberry Corner? Humble, People, Cats. Iykyk.

Their Menu

So this is all the things that Blackberry Corner has to offer. Being a restaurant that specialises in breakfast, their menu include rotis, nasi lemak, mee kari and a bunch of other dishes that will head-start your day.

Review

Mee Kari
Price: RM5


The mee kari tasted like what our grandmas' would cook for us. It is made with love. The curry was thick and creamy, with a perfect hint of spicy. It was full of spices and they put perfect amount of beef on top. The beef was super tender. To make it perfect, squeeze the lime to add a little bif of acidic. Overall, we would rate it 9.5/10!

Lontong
Price: RM3


In any event, the broth of this lontong, which is also cooked with fresh shrimp and combined with the spiciness and fatty flavour of Sambal, has a lot of vegetable elements. You must explore the Blackberry Corner vendor to taste the variety of lontong food.

Mee Sup
Price: RM3.50


For those who prefer something soupy. this is one of the stall that you need to try. The mee sup here is worth to die for with the richness and savoury of the ingredients such as fishballs, boiled egg and the veggies. Solid 7/10.

All in all, aside from the decent food and unignorable cats lounging around in the restaurant, Blackberry Corner has a special spot in our hearts as it is our main go-to breakfast spot as it is literally just around the corner. With that said, we are giving Blackberry Corner the final rating of 4 out of 5 stars! We would give them the full 5 stars if they are open at night.
