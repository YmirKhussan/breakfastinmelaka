Roti Canai Kayu Arang

Background

Funny story, our visit to this hawker stall was actually a last minute Plan-B type beat. We were supposed to try out another well-known restaurant but it was closed when we came so we had to recoup and think of another place to lepak at which turned out to be - Roti Canai Kayu Arang. This stall is known for its myriad of Roti Canai choices and its witty cashier/servicing. We definitely had a great time eating and interacting with the hosts!

Their Menu

Roti Canai Kayu Arang offers one of the most variety of dishes among all the breakfast stalls we have reviewed so far. Their signature dish includes Roti Tampal Dangdud, Roti Sarang Burung, and Roti Dol

Review

Roti Dol
Price: RM3.50


This roti dol is basically roti banjir with 2 gooey eggs on top of it. The roti canai was crispy on the outside and soft on the inside. The gooey eggs on top of it makes it creamy when mixed together. They cooked the roti on charcoal stove, so the roti has a little hint of smoky aroma. It was a new and unique taste for us but we definitely enjoyed it. Overall, we would rate it 8/10!

Roti Sarang Burung
Price: RM6.50


For the price, Roti Canai Kayu Arang's take on the infamous Roti Sarang Burung is one of the best value for your buck. The roti is neither too soft nor too hard. Personally, we loved its sambal as it perfectly complements the whole dish. Lastly, the eggs inside the "Sarang Burung" could be cooked for a little bit longer but that is just my preference.

Lempeng Kelapa
Price: RM4


What else is better than to have Lempeng Kelapa for breakfast? Aside from the sambal and Teh Tarik this dish we had in Dapur Arang was already so good and we would give it a 8/10

Roti Canai Kayu Arang is definitely one of those stalls where even if you eat there once, you'll remember the experience for a long time, if not forever. Everything from food, service, cleanliness, and price is perfect. The only nitpick we had for this hawker stall is probably its long queue times on peak hours. Even still, we are giving Roti Canai Kayu Arang a 5-star rating!
