Cabin Mak Timah

Background

To be brutally honest, we did not plan to try out this stall - we wanted to try the stall next to this one that is infamous for their roti sarang burung but unfortunately it was closed. You win some you lose some. On the bright side though, this stall turned out to be serving some pretty good so thats a win in our book ^_^

Their Menu

Cabin Mak Timah's menu offers basically all you'd expect from a stall that specalises in breakfast with Lontong Darat as their signature dish.

Review

Lontong Kering, mee rebus, meehun sup utara
Price: RM6


This was our first time trying lontong kering, To simplify it, lontong kering is basically nasi impit with combinations of lauk of your own choice. Here, they combine sambal paru, rendang daging, and sambal ikan bilis. When mixed together, it was an explosion of flavours! One thing is, the sambal paru was not cooked perfectly. We prefer crunchy paru but theirs was not crunchy. However the flavors were fantastic so we would rate it 7/10!

Mee Rebus
Price: RM5.50


Mee Rebus with flavours like curry-like gravy and ingredients like yellow egg noodles, which are also used in Hokkien mee. Mee Rebus are not the main menu at this stall, lovers of the dish won't be dissatisfied by the curry sauce's intensity.

Meehun Sup
Price: RM5


I ordered Meehun Sup but I got Mee Sup. However, I cannot complain since their sup is just nice and the mee is not too kenyal and not too keras. It is also completely vegan since it is filled to the brim with veggies and tahus.

Cabin Mak Timah is a modest breakfast stall. And that basically sums up the entire stall. Modest. Place is decent. Food is okay. Service is friendly and quick. Overall a mid experience so we are going to give Mak Timah's Cabin a middle star-rating of 3/5
